About the International Airport of Gjilan

Founded: March 15, 2023
Location: Gjilan, Kosovo
Type: Public International Airport
Operator: Gjilan Aviation Authority (GAA)

The International Airport of Gjilan (IAGJ) was established to connect southeastern Kosovo with the world. Strategically located near the city of Gjilan, the airport acts as a modern travel hub bridging Eastern Europe, Western Europe, and the Middle East.

Since its inauguration in 2023, IAGJ has been committed to innovation, safety, and sustainability. The airport features a 3,800-meter runway capable of handling wide-body aircraft, solar-powered infrastructure, and efficient customs and baggage systems.

With growing passenger demand and regional importance, Gjilan International is more than just an airport — it’s a symbol of progress and connection for the people of Kosovo and the Balkans.
