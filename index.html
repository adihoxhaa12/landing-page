Welcome To International Airport
of Gjilan

The International Airport of Gjilan is a newly developed international gateway located near the city of Gjilan, Kosovo. Strategically positioned in southeastern Kosovo, the airport aims to enhance regional connectivity, support economic growth, and ease air traffic congestion from Pristina International Airport.
Location: The airport is situated approximately 10 kilometers southeast of Gjilan city center, offering convenient access to passengers from southeastern Kosovo, northern Macedonia, and western parts of southern Serbia.
Facilities: Runway: 3,200-meter asphalt runway suitable for all wide-body aircraft
Terminal: Modern passenger terminal with a capacity of 2 million passengers annually
Cargo Center: A dedicated logistics and cargo terminal to boost trade and freight operations
Ground Transport: Shuttle services, taxis, and future rail connections to Gjilan and Pristina
Parking: 1,200-space on-site parking facility for short- and long-term use
Airlines & Destinations: As of its projected opening in 2027, the airport is expected to host a mix of legacy carriers and low-cost airlines, with initial routes to major European hubs such as Vienna, Istanbul, Zurich, and Frankfurt.
Significance: The International Airport of Gjilan is a key infrastructure project for Kosovo’s southeastern region, expected to boost tourism, attract foreign investment, and provide greater mobility for the diaspora. It also plays a role in decentralizing air traffic from the capital, making air travel more accessible for surrounding municipalities.
Development & Ownership: The airport is being developed through a public-private partnership between the Government of Kosovo and international investors, with construction beginning in 2025 and completion expected by the end of 2027.
