About Gjilan International Airport

Gjilan International Airport is a world-class airport located in the heart of the city. We handle over 2 million passengers annually and offer top-tier services to ensure a smooth travel experience.

Services Offered

  • 24/7 Customer Support
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the terminal
  • Duty-Free Shopping & Restaurants
  • VIP Lounge Access
  • Flight Assistance for Seniors and Families

Transportation Options

  • Taxi & Rideshare Pickup Zones
  • Public Transit Connections
  • Airport Shuttle Services
  • Rental Car Center

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What time should I arrive for my flight? – At least 2 hours before domestic and 3 hours before international flights.
  • Is Wi-Fi available? – Yes, it's free throughout the airport.
  • Where can I find lost & found? – In Terminal B, near Gate 14.
